Kyoto Patrol Districts!!!

by shimazuryu

You guys!!!! (jumps up and down) Get this! On page 7 of the guidebook
to the NHK series, I found a map of Kyoto with the different districts
that the different patrol groups ran, as of July 1866!!! I've posted a
sketch of it (I don't have access to a scanner right now) in the
Photos section!!!

Interesting thing of note-- Shinsengumi didn't patrol a large area,
but they DID patrol the area around their HQ which included Shimabara
(w/courtesans and all), which is on the left side of the map I posted,
AND, they patrolled what I think is Gion (the geisha/teahouse
district), which is the one on the right side. Leave it to our guys to
get all the babes....lol

--M.
