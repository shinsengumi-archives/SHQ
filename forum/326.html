I've been meaning to learn html for some time, but knew that it was pointless to even approach it until I was really ready. I suddenly got ready... :-)

It is a LONG way from finished... needs some tweaking in various spots... no manga images yet (just received them a few minutes ago) and no PMK yet (going after permissions tomorrow)... and a couple of links need looking into. I owe many thanks to Tiffany and Kevin for helping me today... especially Tiffany for giving me that little extra push over the side to do it. Comments and complaints welcome.
