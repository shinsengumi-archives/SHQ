Often TB patients die of asphyxiation and during their last moments
gasp for air. This is noted in the novel THE MAGIC MOUNTAIN (cited
in the Okita + TB essay) and also implied in the biography of TB
patient, Emma Dickerman Straight (American teacher at Tokio's
school) when her friend watched as the light left her eyes (Emma
died before her friend found her).
