A little OT: The Hakkenden

by mg_batt

This is an older anime that I've been trying to watch. It's about
eight warriors who are descendents from an inu god and it's set
during the feudal age of Japan. I won't say much but it I thought I
should mention that the story revolves around the "Murasame" and oni
and spirits. The story can be hard to follow sometimes because
there's a lot of symbolization and drifting in/out of reality.
There's also a lot of abuse of power directed towards commoners. Just
thought I should mention it in case anyone is interested, since in
one of the chats the murasame sword was discussed...
