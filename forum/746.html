Progress!

by shimazuryu

OK, I now have a kanji dictionary, and am working on the names I couldn't
decipher. I've managed to get several, and will be steadily continuing work
on the family tree. Most recent addition-- Tokio's aunt Fuku, Tokio's
brother Morinosuke, Tsutomu's daugther, Kyouko, and Saitou's brother-in-law
Soma Yoshiaki, the chief physician to the Mito Tokugawa. Oh, and
interestingly enough, the Numazawa who are related to Tokio are also related
to a Saigou family-- spelled the same way as Saigou Takamori. However, I
doubt it was the same Saigou.

Will continue to keep you all updated!
