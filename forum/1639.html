Tama-san, Kamo-san, otsukaresama deshita^^
(Thank you for showing lovely photographs.)


Although I was not able to see, NHK was broadcasting this festival
by news.
My friend was looking at the news by chance.
She asked me.
"Who is that man other than Hijikata and Shimada?"
Since I was not looking at the news, I do not understand the person.
However, my friend said that he was not a Shieikan member and had a
flag.
Therefore, I said to her, "I think that he is Ozeki Masajirou of the
role putting up the flag of Shinsengumi." .
(He followed Hijikata to Hakodate. Therefore, it is not strange even
if Aizu has him.Incidentally, from formation of Shinsengumi in 1863
to disappearance of Shinsengumi in 1869, only two persons, Shimada
Kai and Ozeki Masajirou, were on the register in Shinsengumi, and
lived to Meiji Era.)
According to the photograph of Dvc00610, my answer seemed to be the
correct answer.^^

Thank you for the again lovely photographs.
