Peacemaker Kurogane has two "seasons"; first is bases off the Shinsengumi Imon Peacemaker manga (or vice versa), and the second one isn't out yet--or if they're even going to turn it into an anime, not sure. Which will most probably be based on the Peacemaker Kurogane manga, the ones licensed by ADV.

The anime has 24 episodes. Imon Peacemaker manga has 6 volumes, and Peacemaker Kurogane manga, I think, has 5 or six volumes.

Hope that helps!

> Re: peace maker kurogane

by jiaxin_nyo

The "second season" is manga only. The anime only has one season, and
it is a mix of both manga, although it uses more more the first
(Shinsengumi Imon Peacemaker). Some plot elements, such as Saitou's
appearance, come from the sceond (Peace Maker Kurogane).
As Kaneyoshi wrote, here are 24 episodes. Currently, ADV has 6 discs
(w/ 21 eps.) out. I'm not sure when the 7th will be out.
Manga-wise though, SIPM is not licensed. If you want to read it, you
can get the scanlations from http://www.mangacity.net PMK is licensed
by ADV, and up to volume 4 is currently out.
