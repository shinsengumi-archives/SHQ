This interesting project by Mr. Joy Ishii called "Samurai Mad Fellows",
tells the story of young Sakurai, living a boring and lonely life in
modern Japan. One evening when he comes home from work he encounters
Toshizo Hijikata, Genzui Kusaka, Hanjiro Nakamura, Shintaro
Nakaoka...historical figures who gave their lives at the collapse of the
government of Edo.
Sakurai now has to explain to them how japanese society works nowadays.
