I have uploaded a pic of Saitou from the YAOI manga Soshite Haru no
Tsuki in the PHOTOS section under "the many faces of Saitou"...I read
the first chapter months ago and it is just yaoi yaoi yaoi....NOW I
just checked the characters section at
http://sanada.from-beneath-you.net/soshite/
and I found it very interesting that Soshite Okita's childhood friend
is the Shogun's Kagemusha. If you have seen Kurosawa's film then
you'll know that it means shadow warrior or body double (stand in) for
the important person. Okita's friend knows Saitou (that is the
mystery part) and I thought yaoi mangas did not have good stories! In
any case Soshite Saitou is very well designed (okay I'll say it he's
*adorable*) and perhaps the few characters in the series -not-
involved in the yaoi activities ^_^
