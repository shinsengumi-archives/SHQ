JSDF is everywhere in the world of anime most noticeably in the
Patlabor 2 directed by Mamoru Oshii (Ghost in the Shell and Jin-
Roh:The Wolf Brigade). Mamoru Oshii is fascinated with how the
police and military function, advanced technology and ofcourse naked
female cyborgs. In Patlabor 2, no naked female cyborgs ^_^ by the
way, the tension between JSDF and the TMPD Mobile Unit (focus of
the film) is addresed and the director makes some pretty straight
forward and highly critical AND controversial comments about
politics, an unjust/just peace, the concept of a just/unjust war and
so forth. I find it fascinating that many anime actually address
the competition(?) and quiet conflict between the police and army
(all of which can be traced back to the Seinan War). Mamoru Oshii
does not show the police and army attacking one another but he does
illustrate the rift between the organizations.
