by warg3791

I recall seeing this a long time ago, but could never find it again
till tonight. Apparently there was a book or a series of some sort
about Hijikata when he was growing up in Ishida (about 15 years old)
called "Baragaki". He was supposed to be a real brat in it. It must
have not gone over so well. Anyway, I finally found some reference to
the thing here:
http://rau.milkcafe.to/makoto/itadaki/book/baragaki.htm

Also, it seems there must be (or there was) some sort of Shinsengumi
card game out there:
http://rau.milkcafe.to/makoto/itadaki/card.htm

This site had some pretty good fanart. Some of it was Takasugi and
I'm not sure who the other guys were. The Hijikata pictures (# 3, 4,
and 7) were very well done. I think 3 edges the other two out a bit
though just from an emotional standpoint. Here's the url:
http://bankara.hp.infoseek.co.jp/bakumatsu.gel.html

And finally I came across reference to something called Hijikata
Toshizo: Shiru no Kiseki. Is this an anime or what?! One pic is
definitely Hijikata in Hakodate. There's no mistaking him:
http://www.catsuka.com/interf/forum/viewtopic.php?t=691&start=72

Unfortunately it is not in English or Japanese.
