Shinsengumi had several colors of uniforms reported by contemporary
accounts, so it's likely that they were rotated. Most commonly, though (in
films) you see blue with white triangles for "common" Shinsengumi, black
with white triangles for Hijikata, and white with black triangles for
Kondou.
