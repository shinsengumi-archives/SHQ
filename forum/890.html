Taking suggestions

by warg3791

As Barb has already mentioned, I've made two quizzes for the
Shinsengumihq.com site. These first two are meant more or less as
test quizzes while I learn exactly how everything works. Now that
I've gotten the hang of it, I'm looking for suggestions! I've already
discussed a quiz with Barb that will be centered around testing a
person's knowledge of the Shinsengumi, especially the odder facts
we've come across. Considering the way things are set up, it will
probably work best as a true or false type quiz. Also, I want to do
improved versions of the test quizzes and a movie/anime type quiz
about the shows the Shinsengumi have appeared in, and a specifically
PMK quiz. I'm looking for suggestions on questions and answers for
all of these.

I'm also willing to do a Sekihoutai quiz if our resident Sagara fan
will be willing to lend a hand with planning it out. Does anyone have
any suggestions other than what's already been listed? And NO, taking
a hike, a long walk off a short pier, and playing in traffice DO NOT
COUNT!!!

Seriously, I want everyone to have a say in these. I feel a group
effort can only improve the quality of what eventually gets posted on
the site. If possible, we can discuss this the next time we schedule
a chat. So go over to Shinsengumihq.com, take the two tests already
on site, and think over what you'd like to see on them. Thanks ahead
of time! Looking forward to hearing what everyone has to say!

-MissBehavin
