was:

Dai-bosatsu Toge printed in 1929

MIRROR MIRROR
http://www.shinsengumihq.com/MirrorMirror.htm

supported Shinsengumihq.com financially and oversaw this list with
her awesome omnipotence and wisdom

I would like to congratulate her on this historical mark in Western
Shinsengumi fandom (^_^)

http://www.shinsengumihq.com
I hope that we fans will help make this work a major success

MIRROR, MIRROR
by Barbara Sheridan

Emiko "Emmi" Maeda didn't plan to spend the last summer vacation
before college kidnaping the future Emperor of Japan. And she didn't
expect to be chased, attacked, or seduced by sword wielding samurai or
put on the marriage market by an ancestor who died a century before
she was born, but she was. And it all started with a shabby old mirror
she found after publicly humiliating herself...
