List of New Pictures

by shimazuryu

OK, I've just posted several new pictures in the "Shinsengumi and
Related People" file. Here's a list:

1. Hotta Masayoshi-- Bakufu (shogunate) senior counselor. Successor to
Abe Masahiro.

2. Miyabe Teizou-- the head of the Choshuu group that met at the
Ikedaya on the night of July 6, 1864.

3. Nariai Kiyoshi-- Unknown, might be a member of the Shinsengumi. I
have to look into him more.

4. Kawai Tsugunosuke-- head of the defenders of Nagaoka Castle in
summer 1868, in the months before Aizu fell.

5. Satou Hikogorou-- Kondou's friend from Hino (now in Tokyo),
possibly from the Shieikan days. Both letters in the "Historic
Documents" section (by Kondou and Okita) are addressed to him.

Hopefully more on the way soon.

--M.
