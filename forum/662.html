Fujita by day is the squinting/smiling Fujita we all know and love.
Saitou the spy is different. Unlike Kenshin he can control his two
"personas".

If you read the timeline at 3-hajime.com there seems to be some
overlapping (or multitasking?) involved with his job in the
police...because it will say "inspector", "inspector probationary
duties" and so on and so forth with slightly different labels.

So in the RK world maybe the police know him as Lt. Fujita but have
absolutely no idea about the other stuff...it be between Lt. Fujita
and Kawaji.
