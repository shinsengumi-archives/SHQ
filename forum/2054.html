Probably, I think that you will be disappointed about this answer.
(My conscience pricks me when I think of it.)

The girl called Yagi Hide does not exist really.
She is the fictitious person of Taiga drama.
The elder brother and younger brother of a name who are called
Hidejirou and Tamesaburou were in the actual Yagi family.
The present Yagi family is the posterity of Hidejirou.

Incidentally, although the old woman was in the Yagi family of a
Taiga drama, she does not exist really in practice, either.
The actual Yagi family is also holding the funeral at the same time
as death of her of Taiga drama.
In practice, it was the funeral of the child of Yagi family.
Probably, other brother would exist in Hidejirou and Tamesaburou.
(Although Japanese television has little regulation compared with a
foreign country, it becomes nervous at a child's death.)
Serizawa Kamo did not help a funeral in the Taiga drama.
However, actual he acted as a visitor's receptionist together with
Kondou.
The scribble which Serizawa wrote to the note remains then still
now.^_^

momoiro-usagi
