Hello everyone,

I'm sure you've all heard about the Miburo Lair game we have going on
LiveJournal, but you may not know about the other game our "guilty
pleasure" called Far Frontier where darn near anything goes and
historic fact is twisted in strange and unusual ways in the pursuit of
fun and alleviation of boredom.
The premise is that Itou Kashitaro and Takeda Kanryuusai escaped their
historic demises in Kyoto and decided to run away and join the
barbarians in the USA. They landed in 1870 in a sleepy little town
called Pagoda Springs Colorado.

Only they never counted on most of their old co-workers and enemies to
get the same idea. We've even had appearances from the great 15th
century warrior Maeda Toshiie and his lovely wife Matsu and even Saizou
the pig from PMK and Bigfoot are known to pop in O_O.

Don't want to be a transplanted Japanese character? We could use long
term and temporary appearances from assorted American townsfolk types.
