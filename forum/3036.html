I just got Hillsborough's Shinsengumi book (and still feel somewhat
violated for paying $30 for a 200 page book) and I notice on page 33
it says that Shimada Kai studied Shinkeito Ryu in Edo, but everywhere
else indicates Shingyoto Ryu- was this just a mistake, or is there
more to it?

As an aside, I found "propensity to kill" 4 times while literally just
flipping to random pages. I would consider that an error on the part
of the editor. A somewhat unforgivable one, in fact. Ouch.

I am finding it an interesting read, though, I don't mind the writing
style.
