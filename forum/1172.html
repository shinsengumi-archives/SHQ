Toshizo Hijikata of Mars

by tamagot93

Here is a little information about Shinsengumi derivative novel.
http://esbooks.yahoo.co.jp/books/detail?accd=31364092
This isn't a fanfiction, but the title of a SF novel recently published in
Japan, called "Toshizo Hijikata of Mars".
It's a kinda space opera, written by well known SF novelist Taira Yoshioka.
The plot is below.

"In fact, Hijikata Toshizo who must have been killed in war in Goryoukaku had
gone to Mars! He made a decision to complete an organization like Shinsengumi
again in order to protect the princess of Mars."

No doubt, an hommage to E.R.Burroughs' classic,"John Carter of Mars"series.
I'll read it before long to follow Hijikata san's activity with Shinsengumi
like people on Mars.:-)
