Saitou Hajime changed "shitaobi"(It is the same as fundoshi.=Classic

He struck it by hand and eliminated wrinkles. And it dried with a
solar light.

This talk is the episode which shows that Saitou was very clean.

shame has an unsightly body.

I like him to the 2nd in the member of Shinsengumi.
By the way, did you see the "Mibugishiden" movie?
The actor who was playing Saitou on that movie is playing Serizawa
Kamo in this drama.
Similarly, the actor who was playing Okita on that movie is performing
Yamanami in this drama.
It is very interesting if the 4th story is seen in consideration of it.

Incidentally, the picture of Saitou of the top of the site is not him.
The picture makes Saitou's son (Fujita Tsutomu)'s photograph the model.
The picture is in Aizu's Byakkotai memorial hall.
The memorial hall wanted to decorate with Saitou's photograph there.
However, the photograph was not able to be got.
(It was not in the Fujita family which is Saitou's posterity, either. )
Then, Saitou's son's photograph was offered.
