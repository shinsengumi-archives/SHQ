Shinsengumi Saitou Hajime no Subete info

by shimazuryu

Well, although I'm very limited on internet usage for awhile (I just
got back from college), I'm working on some things for our group.
Having gone through the beginning of "Shinsengumi Saitou Hajime no
Subete", I can tell you guys some things:

1. The name Fujita Gorou was given to SH by Matsudaira Katamori.
2. One of Tokio's sisters married the son of Sagawa Kanbei, the chief
retainer of Aizu.
3. One of Saitou's grandsons is named Makoto (not THAT makoto,
though).
4. Remember Tatsuo's original family, Numazawa? Well it turns out
that the Numazawa family was already related to Tokio, on her
mother's side.

I'm also working on translating the family tree, which is somewhat
difficult given the amount of "older" characters in the names that
aren't used very much today. As soon as it's done, I'll upload it.

--M.
