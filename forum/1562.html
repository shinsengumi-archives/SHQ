Kyoto in 1864

by tamagot93

In NHK official site, you can see an old map of Kyoto made in 1864 just
before Ikedaya affair.
http://www.nhk.or.jp/taiga/topic/kochizu/main1.html

You can locate some Shinsengumi related places from the legend on the border
on the left-hand.
You can simply place your cursor on the legend and can enlarge it with click.

The legend from top to bottom
*Panoramic view from Kiyomizu temple
*Great Sanjo bridge
*Mibu village: including Mibu Temple, Yagi Estate, Shintoku Temple
*Ponto cho street ( a kind of detached territory of Gion)

Clicking the asagi blue part of the bottom of flag, you can superpose the
actual Kyoto map.
This is part one of the location series which will be continued until part 5.
