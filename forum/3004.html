by shimazuryu

I can gather the specific ryuuha (school) names of the various martial
traditions of Aizu-han, as they appear in the domain encyclopedia "Hanshi
Daijiten"-- however I know for sure that the sons (and daughters!) of Aizu
were taught:

*Several forms of sword
*Archery
*Spear
*Gunnery
*Artillery
*Swimming
*Horsemanship
*Jujutsu
*Tactics
*Naginata

Also some martial arts I have no idea about, like shubojutsu...

I hope that helps, even a little.

-M.
