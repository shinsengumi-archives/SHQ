The irony of looking up images

by sou_ryu_sen14

This is something I found interesting when I was web-serfing for
pictures.
When I typed in "Satsuma" in a search engine I got pictures that
contained mostly oranges and vases. When I typed in "Choshu" I got
pictures of big guys that looked like wrestlers. But when I typed
in "Shinsengumi" I got pages and pages of our favorite swordsmen
group....It looks like that even though the Ishin Shishi won the war,
it's the Shinsengumi that would be remembered, despite the belief
that "the victors write the history". Talk about ironic...The
Shinsengumi at least won the media war.
--A-chan
