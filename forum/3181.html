The painting is of Yamaguchi Jiro (we know him as SAitou Hajime).
Yamaguchi Jiro led the Shinsengumi in the defense of Aizu. Ofcourse
Hijikata Toshizo was the official leader, however due to a foot
injury Yamaguchi had to step up and take charge. Later on after
Hijikata and many SHinsengumi had to depart, Yamaguchi Jiro and a
handful of Shinsengumi stayed behind. Nakajima Nobori mentioned on
the painting that Yamaguchi Jiro had DIED in Aizu---this is ofcourse
innaccurate (^_^). You see "Yama" (mountain) on his jacket.
Yamaguchi Jiro later changes his name to Fujita Goro (his last name)
