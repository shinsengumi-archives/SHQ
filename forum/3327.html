contain 2 images of the Ministry of Justice during the Meiji Era

Now you may think to yourself what does the Min. of Justice have
anything to do with the Shinsengumi?

Well former members of the Shinsengumi Fujita Goro and also Suzuki
Miki (even if he was not posted in Tokyo) knew these buildings very
well. Trainings/meetings were often not held at the local
station---but at the central admin building, the Ministry of Justice
and or the TMPD headquarters because the Min. of Justice was the
"paperwork" center where alot of stuff was sent to for processing.

Other buildings in the files such as the Panorama structure, kabukiza
and sumo ring are one of the most popular places depicted in ukiyo-e
of that era as well.
