NHK Nagakura's mom -- "He's a great son. People come up to me and
say 'Such a cute face only a mother could love'."

SK Okita -- As a mother I need to get this frail boy into a dojo.
That way he'll grow up to be strong just like his father...

PMK Hijikata -- He's such a talented boy. I usually slip in to his
room at night to get that haiku of his. The ladies and I always have
a blast reading that.

NHK Kondou -- I don't know him anymore. I used to be his mother but
ever since his father called him "Samurai", I don't know him
anymore...

RK Saitou -- I SWEAR! That boy is stubborn everytime he plays AKU
SOKU ZAN with his friends. I don't know what to do with him, I keep
telling him it's just play, a make believe but the boy still goes
after his playmates shouting "gatotsu" and pricking them with his
bokken. I'm afraid he won't have friends anymore after this.
