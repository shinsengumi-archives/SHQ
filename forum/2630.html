Nakajima Nobori in the world of doujinshi and fiction

by secretarytocapt3

Nakajima Nobori has never been featured in any popular anime that we
know of and that is very ironic since---technically he was the first
to produce Shinsengumi fanart...

ghost of Nobori "IT IS IRONIC! I'm a member of the Shinsengumi AND
and artist and I'm not even MENTIONED in manga! I can so be a samurai
spokesman for the NRA too!"

sec "shush, let me continue"

[for new folks] This is his page (link to the gallery of his work is
near the top)
http://www.shinsengumihq.com/Nakajimabio.htm

I was at http://www.hajimenokizu.com and found this:
http://hajimenokizu.com/doujinshi/album22/shinsen_3

which is based on Nakajima Nobori's "portrait" of Hijikata:
http://1happyturtle.com/makoto/portraits/NNHijikataxl.jpg
[click on image for full size version]

but wait there is more, according to a post at a Japanese messageboard
there was a novel featuring Nakajima Nobori
(so I went to our handy dandy
http://www.shinsengumihq.com/ShinsengumiNames.htm and grabbed his name
and stuck it in http://www.google.co.jp)

and so I found (the book at the top is about Nobori, the book right
under it is about Okita):
http://www.jidai-show.net/jdre0202.html

[summary] Nobori dishes out some generous servings of whoop @ss and
for some weird reason there is an [AIZU] connection? ::shrugs:: I can
only guess at the true meaning of the summary (^_^)

ghost of Nobori "just because I paint and enjoy gardening doesn't mean
I'm a wimp"
sec "yes, sure whatever you say"

If anyone finds a shrine to Nobori as they surf Shinsengumi sites
please let me know (^_^)
