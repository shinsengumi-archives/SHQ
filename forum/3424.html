street views up on Google Maps for Japan

by spiritus_saitou

Admittedly, I haven't spent much time with this yet, but thought I'd
pass it along to those who are explorers at heart and, like me, can't
afford to fly across the bigger pond to Japan.

I think Google may have this going for other cities in the world. Well,
now they have it up for the Tokyo area, Kyoto/Osaka/Kobe, Sendai,
Hakodate, Otaru & Sapporo. The little camera icons on the map show
where there are street views available. Once you're on a street level
map, whenever you click on a point on that map, it'll take you to that
point visually.

http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&hl=en&geocode=&q=&layer=c&ie=UTF8&ll=38.\
255436,138.713379&spn=8.605754,16.962891&z=6
