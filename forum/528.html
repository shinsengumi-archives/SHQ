I know from meeting with international students before that a majority
of younger Japanese have pretty darn good English comprehension
skills. Many have studied English since elementary school 0_o and can
read/write basic English so that is the rational behind my objectives
we need to reach out across to the Pacific to our brothers and sistas
who are -even- more serious about their Shinsengumi than we are:) We
also would like Japanese agents to give us a heads up on anything
Shinsengumi related in the media/manga etc.

We need Pro-Ishin ShiShi folks....it's just not the same without
opponents...just feel so obsolete. Remember, as an anime fan I have
no problem with more RK, PMK fans but we have to try to lure in other
folks too so we can -use- them errr learn from them to enrich
fanfiction I mean contribute to our understanding of the
history/politics of the time.

If anyone knows about an upcoming convention with Shinsengumi
cosplaying team or something just invite them to join. Or if anyone
knows someone on the "inside" of a convention which can get us some
free advertising lemme know too. I think we did more than enough to
"season" the board with amazing threads and I bow deeply to everyone
who has taken the time to post...I just have the fate of
www.Saitou-hajime.com in the back of my mind.
