Advance rumor

by spiritus_saitou

Well, I've been waiting all day to hear from Chris or his manager for clarification on this, but... From a message from Chris Patton to his fan group:

>>Also, ADV announced Peacemaker, which I believe I will have a role in, though of course nothing can be officially confirmed or denied as of yet. (wink wink).<<

Is there another anime by that name or is it what I think?

Small world... I "met" Chris through livejournal and when he discontinued his personal journal a few weeks ago (which was a major bummer because it was always such a delight to read), I joined his yahoogroup fan group to keep tabs on what he was up to. (He's extremely personable and in tune with his fans, but I've had no desire to pelt him with e-mails of "so what's new?"... you know what I mean? It was different when it was through lj.) Anyway, if he is talking about PMK, I'll see if he or his manager will keep me up to date so I can pass it on here.

If you're as unfamiliar with Chris' work as I was (and still am, really, since I'm not into anime in any big way), check the CrystalAcids entry for him at http://www.crystalacids.com/database/actor/patton_christopher.html. He was lead character in "Pretear" and "Spriggan" and did several episode characters for "Saiyuki." He's done quite a bit of work and just keeps getting busier...
