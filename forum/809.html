Re: [SHQ] Tech Question (Word img>>>Jpeg)

by spiritus_saitou

Are you able to highlight the image within the document? (You should be able to)

With the image highlighted, right click.

One of your choices is (or should be... Photo Editor must be installed probably) "Photo Editor Photo Object"

Choose "Edit"

That should dump the image into Photo Editor where you can save as a .jpg or other file.

If you need to clean the images, it'll have to go into MS Paint after it's saved as a .jpg.

Let me know off-list if this doesn't work... I'm constantly futzing around with images in MS.

phil
