Hellos and First Impressions.

by Mirel

Konnichi wa minna-san!
I'm new here and I'm totally excited to finally get myself in this group (I
was having arguments with Yahoo about the existence of my account). I am
really interested in learning all I can about the Shinsengumi and its
members, and I've heard this is THE PLACE to get and share info in English.
Just as an intro, I'll give a quick rundown of what I'm into, ne? Let's
see, my fav. member is Saitou Hajime, although Peacemaker Kurogane has
gotten me quite curious about Harada Sanosuke as well. As for anime, I've
seen Kenshin and PMK and I am digging for more. In manga, I've read a
bunch of PMK and I have a bunch of scanlations sitting on my computer
waiting for attention. As for live action stuff, this is where I am
horribly deficient, but I hope to fix that soon!
I've also taken two years of Japanese and would be going into my 3rd year
if it weren't for the fact that I'm going to Tanzania this fall. I love
learning about other aspects Japanese history outside of the Shinsengumi
and the areas I'm most interested in include the Sengoku jidai (age of
civil wars), the Edo period, and the Meiji Restoration.
Also, I'm currently working on a full Shinsengumi uniform (minus the armor,
because that would be really hard to make) to wear to this year's Otakon in
Baltimore, so any tips on such would be greatly appreciated.

Well that's about it!
I look forward to many more e-mails wrought with Shinsengumi goodness!

Tootles!
