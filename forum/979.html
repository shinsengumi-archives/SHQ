AHQ <animationhq@...> wrote:
To:
From: "AHQ"
Date: Sat, 5 Jun 2004 02:33:11 -0400
Subject: [English_Voice_Actors_of_Dubbed_Anime] Wendee Lee chats with AnimationHQ.net!

Wendee Lee makes her triumphant return to AnimationHQ for a third chat
session! With such a variety of characters as Faye Valentine of "Cowboy
Bebop", Angel in "Big O", Charlotte of "Vampire Hunter D: Bloodlust", Miho
in "Witch Hunter Robin", Yumi and Yahiko in "Rurouni Kenshin", Umi
Ryuuzaki/Princess Emeraude of "Magic Knight Rayearth", Rumi of "Perfect
Blue", Gyokuyou/Kourin of "Twelve Kingdoms" and Takako from "Chobits" just
to name a few, Wendee is excited to come and talk to us about her latest
role- Kiva in "Megas XLR" (now airing on Cartoon Network).

The chat session will take place on Saturday, June 5th at 2 pm EST. For
further information on Wendee and how to chat with her, you can visit
AnimationHQ Events page at
http://www.AnimationHQ.net/v2/features/events.htm.
