SHQ Website Restructuring (need your ideas!)

by secretarytocapt3

Hello everyone!

I wanted to present the basic structure of the new SHQ site. SHQ
will be hosted by http://alohaworld.com/ and watched over by an
administrator.

The site will be very simple and will not have dozens upon dozens of
pages with info. The bulk of the history will be in book form you
can download as a pdf file. If we have an update to the book then
there will be an update notice.

I would really, really, really appreciate your opinion and
suggestions. Please send me your ideas secretarytocapt3(at)
hotmail.com by Friday August 8th.

This is really a labor of love, but it's for everyone here so please
contribute and tell us what's on your mind (^_^).

The site will be split like this:

I. Index Page

1) news, updates, links, and sponsor
info

------------------------------------
II. History

1) page which contains pdf file for the e-book (this e-book will
contain alot of info including material covering Aizu)

2) time line
a) General timeline before Bakumatsu Era
b) 1861-1870
b) 1871-1880
c) 1881-1890
d) 1891-1912
e) Post Meiji
------------------------------------
III. Fiction

1) Gallery (cosplay pics, fanart, etc)
2) Fiction (or links to complete fanfiction)
3) Shinsengumi Filmography (by Michael from ninjadojo.com)

------------------------------------
IV. Makoto

1) Makoto Multimedia Dojo (fan audio, music vids etc)

Thanks for reading.
