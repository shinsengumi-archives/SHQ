If (IF) I get some free time sometime in August
(hopefully), I can try making some summaries of some
Shinsengumi movies I have:

- Shinsengumi Onitaicho ("The Demon Captain of the
Shinsengumi"), 1854, 119 min, featuring Kataoka
Chiezo, Nakamura Kinnosuke, Tsukigata Ryunosuke and
others. I guess if someone asks momoirousagi about
those actors, most probably she'll know all of them :D

- Shinsengumi Shimatsuki ("The Record of the Beginning
and the End of the Shinsengumi"), 1963, 94min,
featuring Ichikawa Raizo as Yamazaki Susumu

- Shinsengumi, 1970, featuring Mifune Toshiro,
Nakamura Kinnosuke, Kitaoji Kin-ya, Mikuni Rentaro and
others.

- Shinsengumi TV Asahi soap opera(2 parts), 1987,
featuring Matsukata Hiroki as Kondo Isami and
Shonentai's Higashiyama Noriyuki as Okita Soji.

- Bakumatsu Junjo-den - Okita Soji wa B-Cup ("Tales of
Romantic Adventures in the End of Shogunate - Okita
Soji was a B-cup") :D This is a HILARIOUS movie, where
Okita Soji is a girl (a rather beautiful B-cupped one
:D), who Sakamoto Ryoma tries to get :D

I have two more movies on the Shinsengumi, but they
are in my (now deceased) master's house, and I have to
find time to go there and retrieve them.

But before that, I still owe Tiffany that info on the
Tenma-ya Inn affair... :(
