> Re: [SHQ] NHK Shinsengumi site limit (and how to save flash)

by tamagot93

They have already moved the Shinsengumi site to
http://www3.nhk.or.jp/taiga/shinsen_gumi/
and the former address is linked to "Yoshitsune" (Hero of the next year
indeed) one now.
So they are still keeping our hero's site and there is no reason to get
up a panic. (or it's only me who has been panicked?)

As for the saving flash, there is a free and practical one here,
http://www.vector.co.jp/soft/dl/win95/art/se175326.html
called FLASH MANIA, but the problem is this one has only Japanese
language explanation.

However, I try to put some explanation as this one is quite simple to use.
At first you put the link above to any translation site to find the
download button.

And once the soft is downloaded, and installed, started, you can see the
black screen with some buttons below.

When you click on the button "S" of the search, you can see the list of
flashes you've downloaded. (So you can download at first any flash
normally on your browser) Then you can choose the flash you wish save and
you right clicked the chosen file. There are two option in Japanese but
you can see at least (S) for save and (D) for delete.
Choosing (S) option you can save the file where you want on your PC.
And the button "O" is open and "M" is misc.

May be I explain badly and it seems complicated, but once you do try it,
it would be more simple and easy, I think.
And I don't know for the Mac, sorry.

Tama
