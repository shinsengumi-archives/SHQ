Final register of the Tokugawa system

by shimazuryu

http://www.isics.u-tokyo.ac.jp/db/contents/item.32.N002.html

Final register of the last status of the Tokugawa Shogunate in the Keio era.

Here's what I can tell from the middle column:

1. Head of the Genji (Minamoto) clan (Genji no Choja), Shogun (Sei-i
Taishogun), 1st court rank, Udaijin (Right Minister), Prince Minamoto
Iemochi. Income-- 8 million koku. (As well as some other things I can't
quite make out).

2. 3 main branches of the Tokugawa:

Owari (then something I can't read)-- last head of Owari was Yoshikatsu,
Katamori's birth brother.

Kii Chunagon (Middle Chancellor of Kii Province) Mochitsugu.

Mito Chunagon Yoshiatsu, eldest son and successor to Nariaki.

3. 3 sub-branches of the Tokugawa:

Tayasu Kamenosuke Iesato (later adopted by Yoshinobu, became 16th head of
the main Tokugawa house, died in 1940)

Hitotsubashi Chunagon Yoshinobu (Hitotsubashi Middle Chancellor)

Shimizu (nothing else)-- last head of Shimizu was Tokugawa Atsumori.
Remember, Kondou's father in law was a retainer to the Shimizu lord.

4. Fudai-gashira (Supervisors of Vassal Domains)

Matsudaira Chuushou (Lieutenant General) Higo no Kami Katamori (whom we all
know and love ^_^)

Ii (somethingorther I can't read)

Matsudaira Shoushou (somethingorother) no Kami (can't read the rest)

---

Thought it might interest you all. That's a new role of Katamori-sama's that
I wasn't aware of...

-M.
