Great news!

by shimazuryu

I found out that there's a scanner available on my campus for FREE!
Soo....expect several scans as early as Monday(hopefully)! These include:

1. A never-before seen photo of Matsudaira Katamori, which shows how truly
young he was in those days.
2. Better-quality pictures of Tokugawa Yoshinobu
3. Scans from the official NHK "Shinsengumi!" Guidebook
4. Pictures of Nijo and Aizu Castles
5. My own drawings, including Hiro and Yuki (from my Shinsengumi novel), as
well as my own depictions of Shinsengumi members, such as Kondou, Hijikata,
Okita, Saitou, Shimada, and Nakajima Nobori.

Of course, this will all be put up bit by bit. But I just wanted to let
everyone know that there's a great deal that's on its way.

--Masayoshi
