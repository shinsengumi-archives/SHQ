A manga about Shinsengumi in the North

by tamagot93

I come across this manga called , "Hokuso Shinsengumi"created by Kanno Aya,
in a bookshop today.

The title can be translated as," Shinsengumi running in the North."and as we
can guess
from the title, it relates the story around Hijikata in the North and some
members of Shinsengumi of late period.

This manga is categorized as Shojo but the drawing is very stylish and story
isn't typically shojo at all.
I liked the story of Souma Kazue very much.
