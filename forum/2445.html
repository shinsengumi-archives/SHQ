Sequel - NHK Shinsengumi!

by serizawakamo

A rough translation of the news...

----------------

A sequel for "Shinsengumi!" announced, to be aired
next January = NHK

NHK has announced on the 25th that it will be airing
in January, as a historical soap opera for New Year's,
a one-shot soap opera showing what happened after the
ending of last year's long run historical soap opera
("taiga dorama") "Shinsengumi!". It is the first time
that a sequel for historical soap operas is made.

As for the soap opera, it will feature the same cast
of the original work, including Koji Yamamoto playing
the lead role as Toshizo Hijikata. The script will be
written by Koki Mitani. It hasn't been announced the
day and time that it will be aired.
