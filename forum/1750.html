Not sure about all the kanji, so I can only read part of it....

Page 33-- "Tsuma Tokio no Ie" ("(his) Wife, Tokio's Family"

Regarding the wedding---

Hon-nakoudo (Chief go-between): Matsudaira Katamori.
Shimo-nakodo ("Lesser" go-between): Yamakawa Hiroshi

(not sure) something about Sagawa Kanbei "starting" something.

Matsudaira's adopted sister Teru-hime was involved.

Takagi clan were major retainers of the Aizu Matsudaira-- with a stipend of
300 koku.

Tokio's father Kojuurou was born in 1818.
