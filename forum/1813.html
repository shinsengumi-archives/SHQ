Hi!

If you check back about a month in the list archives, I asked a similar question before. Friendly people gave me a lot of help then, so those posts contain quite a bit about Hijikata's early life, including name and age of all his siblings.

Would also be interested in siblings' offspring though... I know he must have had at least one nephew, since there's still a Hijikata family in the Hino area who claim kinship with him. Also it's likely that any children would've come through his second eldest brother, Kiroku. As far as I remember it the eldest one, Tamajirou, was blind and hence disqualified from inheriting, and probably hard to marry off. The other brother I think was adopted by another family and changed his name.
