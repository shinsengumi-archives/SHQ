PMK licensed by ADV?

by mg_batt

You probably already know.. But for those who do not..

Well I was just over at animenewsnetwork forum and was I surprised!
Geez go away for some weeks and miss out on things... ^_^*

ADV has announced that it will license 1000 volumes (not titles) of
manga, so far 37 had gone through and this includes Peacemaker
Kurogane. I could not find reference to whether they will be
translating the prequel SIMP but translation of the second part PMK
is ongoing.

About the Anime, ADV (at least from what I know right now) has not
announced ownership of the Anime but there are rumors that they are
or very soon will be dubbing the series... Wow that was fast. If
this is true then I'm glad I got the fansub version first. Unless
ADV announces they officially licensed PMK Anime then I guess the
distribution of fansubs by fansubbers won't stop till then. So glad
though that someone is picking up the manga because that means the
DVDs will be out soon, probably on or after fall this year.
WAI!!! :O)
