Since someone asked for the translation... here it
goes a rough translation... sorry for the many typos
and mistakes, but I've been in a bit of hurry these
days, so sorry ^^;;;


http://www.geocities.co.jp/HeartLand-Cosmos/4774/page015.html

When Miki returns to Edo, Toudou Heisuke from the
Shinsengumi invites him to join the Shinsengumi. In
the 1st year of the Genji Era, he joins the
Shinsengumi, along with his elder brother.

At this time, both change their names: the elder
brother names himself as Kashitarou and the younger
brother as Mikisaburou.

He was the Captain of the 9th Division and was also
involved in the Intelligence.

Having the fief of Satsuma as their supporter, he
becomes a Kinri Goryo Eji.

After the murder of his elder brother, on November
18th, he stays at the fief house of Satsuma, planning
the murder of Kondou.

On December 9th, he is one of the people who shoots
Kondou while Kondou was walking at Sumizome, in Kyoto,
but they manage only to wound Kondou, without killing
him.

After taking part in the War of Toba and Fushimi, he
became the Captain of the 2nd Division of the
Sekihoutai.

He tried many things to exact revenge upon the
Shinsengumi, but he found himself in the new era
without fulfilling his wishes.

He became a policeman in the Meiji Era, and when he
was an Officer at the Tsuruoka police, he spent all
his money to build a hospital (hospitals?)

After that, he spent the rest of his life in Ishioka,
his homeland, living till the 8th year of the Taisho
Era, dying at the age of 83.

------------

That's more or less what the fishy part says...
