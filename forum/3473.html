Thrilled to announce...

by secretarytocapt3

FALLING THROUGH GLASS
by Barbara Sheridan
a work of historical fiction published by Liquid Silver Books
which will first be released in e-book format on Sept 28th.

So keep an eye on this page: http://www.liquidsilverbooks.com/

Book trailer:
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Bd4WgfyKgFA
While the trailer doesn't show the Shinsengumi rest-assured the guys
are in the book.

I can't wait for this book to come out in paperback format but I admit the e-book format is sooo affordable. FALLING THROUGH GLASS is a classy mxf romance with the right balance of action and adventure. I enjoyed reading the early drafts, under the title of Mirror, Mirror, of this work but the FINAL FORM in which this work has taken is FABULOUS FOLKS!

:: SQUEEEEEEE ::

So one of our very own SHQ members will be the first non-Japanese
to publish a work of historical fiction featuring the Shinsengumi.

I look forward to fulfilling my goals with SHQ but at the same
time I'm sorting being pulled in different directions too.
