Fujita Tsutomu's birth

by shimazuryu

Yamakawa Hiroshi, the former Aizu retainer who was a close friend of Saitou
Hajime (Fujita Gorou), wrote a poem on the occasion of Goro's son Tsutomu's
birth. I can't quite make out the meaning-- it plays off his name
"tsutomu"-- which means "to work [hard]," and exhorts him to grow into a
distinguished man, but I wanted to share it in transliterated form.

Tsutometefu
Na ni somukasu wa
yakate yoni
takaku tsutomu no
tatasareme ya wa

Found it on http://o.z-z.jp/b/zaneika/log/20.html

-M.
