I hope these scans of PMK Saitou will be useful for those of you who
do fanart/fanfic because even if you saw the anime you may not have

(Nagakura even apologizes for saying this)...and the whole bit with
the notion of a 2nd movement (ninja like move)...lastly there is a
tiny part that points out to the audience that Saitou was literally in
the background of various scenes...you just don't notice it. I also
happen to love PMK Saitou's hair :) and how Nanae draws people's hair.
