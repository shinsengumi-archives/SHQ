Hi Yuki-san!

you can use Internet Explorer and steal it out of the Temporary Items
folder
(Home folder) ~/Library/Preferences/Explorer.Temporary files
- wait til the animation has loaded, then do not close the explorer
window! and go grab the swf file out of the folder. Most files open in
Safari, IE or Quicktime Player.
Some, however, do not -_-;

But there are ways to work around that.

In Safari, open page, let it load, go to the menu, "view source" and
search for (find) ".swf". Then paste the address into the browser
window, err.. how to explain that... well, in place of whatever was
behind the last slash on the page...
then "save as", and it should save it as swf file.

Or send me the URL and I'll try to grab it for you
