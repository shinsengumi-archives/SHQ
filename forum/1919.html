Kojima Shikanosuke is the leading farmer of Onoji village, and is
the sponsor of Tennen Rishin Ryuu.
And he was Hijikata's distant relative.
(They are not blood relation. The Hashimoto families which are
Hijikata's grandmother's parents' home were Kojima and a relative.)
Kojima Shikanosuke was a farmer and a scholar.
There were many books in his house.
Kondou and Hijikata borrowed and studied the book from him.
Their thought has the large influence from Kojima.
After becoming Meiji Era, Kojima collected the data about
Shinsengumi.
They are preserved carefully and are in the Kojima data hall which
his posterity manages now.

http://www.ceres.dti.ne.jp/~kojishir/

Moreover, he edited a book called "Ryoyuuden", in order to restore
the honor of Kondou and Hijikata.
