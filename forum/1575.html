Full Speed Ahead: Fuun Bakumatsu Den! Sequel to Fuun Shinsengumi

by mg_batt

Not sure if this has been posted before, been out of the loop again!
Either way a sequel to Fuun Shinsengumi was announced by Genki for
release in Japan this winter 2004.

I believe the first one was where you can play as one of the
Shinsengumi to guard the bakufu against some tenchu ninja's whose
sole purpose in life is to assasinate...

This one is a little different the roles are reversed, you get to
play as samurais out to assasinate the bakufu and you'll be pitted
against the "our" guys and some others pro shogunate. Ishin lovers
will fight with Ryoma and Takasugi...

What are the improvements? Well better graphics and now it actually
has voices.. Also you are not restricted to just Kyoto anymore...
