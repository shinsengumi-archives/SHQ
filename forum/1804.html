This says that April 16, 1863, Yamanami, Saitou, Nagakura, and Okita went to
the Kyoto Shogoshoku/Aizu headquarters at Kurotani (Konkomyoji Temple) and
demonstrated their kenjutsu before Lord Matsudaira.

Then, Inoue Matsugorou, Genzaburou's older brother, came to visit on April
17th and talked with Hijikata, Okita, Genzaburou, and Saitou about Kondou's
"pride" (being too full of himself).
