It is a charcoal sketch...done with soft charcoal so it does look like
a photo...there are bigger versions of this image with writing on the
side and his name "Saitou Hajime" even though he was supposed to go by
Yamaguchi Jirou/o by the time this portrait was done. 1to5.net has a
mini essay which debunks the charcoal sketch portrait we have gotten
used to.

If the man identified in the group photo is S.H. then -wow- either the
artist who did the charcoal sketch was WAY off or perhaps someone
accidently wrote in the wrong name on the wrong portrait...he is SO
unattractive LOL...what a contrast to Tokio who looks very elegant in
the photo with their son :) well I'm not a model myself:)

My Japanese books came with lots of inserts and advertisements listing
Shinsengumi books and on one ad it actually cropped the group photo to
show the REAL S.H....so it seems that the marketing is also changing
to address what researchers have found rather than go with the image
which is all over the internet.
