Youkoso!

by shimazuryu

Youkoso, minna-sama! On behalf of everyone on the Miburo list, I would
like to welcome all Miburo veterans and new members to SHQ! Though
"our" guys may have departed from this world in a blaze of glory, they
have been condemned to relative ignominy. It would seem, however, that
the Japanese public is rediscovering their value, and we who are
outside of Japan have learned of their reknown. This group will, among
other things, help to further the knowledge of the Shinsengumi in the
world. Although, as the description indicated, we also enjoy simply
being Shinsengumi otaku...^_^

--Shimazu Masayoshi

P.S. Irony of ironies, huh? A Shimazu in a Shinsengumi group...but
we'll leave that for the historical details that'll be posted soon.
