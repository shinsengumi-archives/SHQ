Harada & Nagakura

by warg3791

Great duos are always inspiring. Batman and Robin. Lewis and Clark.
Chip and Dale...

According to fictional sources at least, the great duo of the
Shinsengumi appears to have been Harada and Nagakura. Movies and manga
often shown in association with one another. Historically this is
slightly backed up by the fact they were both on the list of those
protesting against Kondo and by the fact that they both left the
Shinsengumi after a disagreement with their boss following the defeat
at Kofu.

So the question is, what is your favorite portrayal of the two? What
was your least favorite protrayal? And of all those who have (or could)
play the roles of the 2nd and 10th captains, what actors would you most
like to see bringing this famous friendship to life?
