"Shinsengumi" 2000 http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0400817/
is an animated movie...I think "stop-motion" with paper
dolls?...Kodachiko Kureru's site has some pics of this unique
film...it's based on a manga by Hiroshi Kurogane. I ordered this book
and it hasn't arrived yet.

Kodachiko's site (there is a pic of paper doll Saitou too by the way)
http://www.angelfire.com/anime2/shinsengumi/kuroganemovie.html

The thing that is driving me insane is
1) KON ICHIKAWA directed it!!! My fav films from him are THE KEY and
BURMESE HARP...Kon Ichikawa has a background in animation so this film
must've been fabulous

2) Kiichi Nakai does the voice of Hijikata Toshizo!
For those of you who haven't seen MIBUGISHIDEN...Kiichi Nakai is the
actor who portrays Yoshimura Kanichiro (I spelled the name wrong).
Maybe this actor is a closet Shinsengumi fan:)

Why oh why is this film not available for purchase!!!!
