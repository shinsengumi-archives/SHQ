Okay so I don't know if the real Hijikata sang but I just got to see
episode 20 of the NHK Shinsengumi and Serizawa cheezed some important
person off to the point that they were going to leave the gathering
Kondou threw for them. To lighten up the situation Kondou prods Hijikata
into singing some popular song of the day while he (Kondou) and Inoue
Genzaboru do some dance. Nagakura and Harada join in the dance too
(Harada taking yet another chance to show of his fundoshi--bright yellow
this time). I'm guessing it was a popular song of the day because two of
the little kamuro girls in attendance wit their mistress sang along with
part of it.

Anyway, that boy can sing!!! It's a catchy little tune too, I just wish
the darn vcr was working properly to have taped it.

In other events, Yamanami, Todou and Saitou & Okita were in charge in
signing up new recruits. One guy was a total boob and Todou knocked him
out with a small tap tot eh head. The next guy that came took Saitou on
_Barehanded_ and won!!!!! Saitou was crushed and quickly went off to
sulk alone. Apparently the boob knows the hand to hand combat guy which
would explain his lack of sword skills if he's also a judo specialist.

Forgive me for not being able to name them as they spoke so fast and I
don't have subtitles here since I get the show on satellite direct from
NHK..
