Entering shyly...

by firuzekhanume2002

Hi...
I suppose it's time to introduce myself... *fidgets slightly*
By now, some of you already know that all time RK favorite character is Enishi, and might wonder what I'm doing here. Hell, I even feel myself like some kind of intruder... However, I think it's not exactly a mystery that my second favorite character in the series is Saitou, and that's why I tend to give him an important role in my fics. So, well, I find tempting any place where I can find interesting information as well as to hear interesting discussions about him, his past and the Shinsengumi. So here I'm.
Yet, there is a second reason, since I am in love with PMK series. The guilty party here is composed by Barbara Sheridan and Miss Behavin *points at both* and their deliciously funny Mibu Times/Dispatch. I found that to understand certain jokes I had to know about PMK, so I decided to download several episodes. Since then, I'm lost in addiction. *wails*
A wave of sadness enveloped me over the past weekend, after I saw episode 23, since it announced that next one would be the ending. Is there any news on any kind of continuation? Is there any place where I can read/download scanlations/translations of the manga? I live where the world ends, in deep South America, so you can imagine, no dreams of getting any copy of the manga at all for a very long time. Any info will be deeply appreciated.
Firuze
