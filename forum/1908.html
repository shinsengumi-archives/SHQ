The name at the top post is "Izuminokami Kanesada" which was the maker of Hijikata's blade. From what my translator picked up, the poster was able to visit someone who owned a Kanesada blade made by the 11th Kanesada.

As for the overseas auctions/sales, there are currently two Kanesada blades for sale here in the U.S.

Aizu Kanesada wakizashi:
http://www.artswords.com/aizukanesada.htm

Izuminokami Kanesada daito:
http://www.artswords.com/aizukanesada.htm

The daito has appraisal papers, but they are not translated like the ones on the wakizashi.


~Okita
