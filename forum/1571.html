I was surprised that Shinsengumi fan are in USA.

If you would like to know whole of real history of Shinsengumi,
I recommend that you try to read Japanese books as below.
Most site of Shinsengumi in Japan include fictional stories
purposefully or in a blind way.

<For high-researchers>
Title: Rekishi no naka no Shinsengumi (Shinsengumi in historical
scope)
Author: Masato Miyaji
Publisher: Iwanami Syoten
http://www.amazon.co.jp/exec/obidos/ASIN/4000025317/qid=1094572756/sr
=1-1/ref=sr_1_10_1/249-9112977-4510712

<For researchers>
Title: Shinsengumi
Author: Rei Matsuura (Director of National Museum of Japanese
History)
Category: Iwanami bunko
Publisher: Iwanami Syoten
http://www.amazon.co.jp/exec/obidos/ASIN/4004308550/qid=1094572563/sr
=1-1/ref=sr_1_10_1/249-9112977-4510712

<For kids and beginners>
Title: Shinsengumi no dai-jyoushiki (Common knowledge of Shinsengumi)
Supervisor: Sayuri Yaguchi (Researcher and Curator of Hino-City
Furusato
museum who have personal familiarity with descendants of shinsengumi
warriors, for example Toshizo Hijikata)
Publisher: Popula-sha
http://www.amazon.co.jp/exec/obidos/ASIN/4591078345/qid%
3D1094573309/249-9112977-4510712

Please share the above information to interested people or other
sites if
you could.
This thing is less well known in Japan also.


Regards
Brio (Satoshi Oishi)
