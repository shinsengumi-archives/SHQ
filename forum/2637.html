A few doujinshi pages ^_^

by shenlong71328

Greetings to all the Shinsengumi lovers of this world! ^_________^

Alright then, these are 8 pages I did because I was deeply inspired (and hooked) on a fanfic I'm currently reading called "The Courtship of Lady Tokio" by Misaki Toyodome. Now, Misaki didn't ask me to draw this, I did it on my own out of pure urge to see what this one particular scene would look like. I was extreamly nervous when I sent her the links to the pages and I am overly excited that she loves them. She loves them so much that she suggested I share these pages with the SHQ mailing list (BTW are the SHQ & Spy division under the same e-mail address for the mailing list?). So, without further adue, the small section of fanfic turned doujinshi:

http://www.deviantart.com/view/24970162/
http://www.deviantart.com/view/24970267/
http://www.deviantart.com/view/24970380/
http://www.deviantart.com/view/25006043/

http://www.deviantart.com/view/25256502/
http://www.deviantart.com/view/25275526/
http://www.deviantart.com/view/25394256/

If any of you haven't read this fic, you should. It is exquisitly writen. The fanfic can be found both on fanfiction.net (http://www.fanfiction.net/s/2049030/1/) & on hakubaikou.com ... the one on FF.net is more far ahead then the one on Hakubaikou's site. Anyways, I hope you guys all enjoy this as much as Misaki did (I know secretarytocapt3 enjoys the fic ^.^). So, 'till later!

-Bunneh/ WhiteRabbit/ Eeni
