> RE: [SHQ] Biographical Dictionaries

by shimazuryu

Not sure. The full-blown serious work "Meiji Ishin Jinmei Jiten" has just
about everyone you could think of from the Bakumatsu and then some,
including Shinsengumi, but as far as a more compact version of what you've
just referenced I'm not sure. There are some websites that have archived
what seem to be full rosters and a great deal of bios, but as I have not had
the chance to look through all of them end to end I can not say if they are
thorough.

-M.
