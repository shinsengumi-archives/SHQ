TREASURE TROVE of Edo and Meiji-era information

by shimazuryu

You guys!! I can't believe I found this!

http://cork.wul.waseda.ac.jp/kosho/

Seems to be run by Waseda University-- a HUGE archive of newspapers and
prints relating to the late Edo/Bakumatsu and early Meiji periods!

In particular, this section of the archive:

http://cork.wul.waseda.ac.jp/kosho/bunko10

Has info on Seinan War/Saigou Takamori, starting from b10_8328 to b10_8338.
My favorite is
http://cork.wul.waseda.ac.jp/kosho/bunko10/b10_8336/b10_8336.jpg, a picture
of two of the samurai women of Satsuma, armed with naginata and short
swords.

--M.
