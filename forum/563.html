IN MEMORY OF A GREAT MAN

by warg3791

Well, this is the 25th of April. The day Kondou Isami was executed by
the Ishin. I think it would be appropriate for everyone to take a
moment to reflect on this great man's life. After all, he WAS the
Commander of the group we've all come to love and admire. Without
him, the Shinsengumi would never have existed. To some he was a
teacher and a friend. To others he was a leader and a source of
inspiration. To many he was an enemy not to be taken lightly. But in
the end he was just a man who never surrendered his ideals and was
willing to die for what he believed. For that he has my deepest
respect always.

May your memory never fade from this world, Kondou-sama.

-MissBehavin
