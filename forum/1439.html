Shinsengumi RPG - Lair of the Miburo

by warg3791

We're starting an RPG on the LiveJournal called Lair of the Miburo.
It will take a bit for the game to get started, because as of yet we
don't even have a background story or anything. We will be doing a
brief chat session on the message board around 9:00pm EST to try to
nail this down. Feel free to join in the discussion.

Also, I need anyone interested to e-mail me, Barb, or phil and tell
us the character you want to be. You MUST contact one of us because
the membership into the RPG is restricted to invitation only in order
to prevent passers-by from making pointless entries. Hurry, because
the main men will go fast. We also need people to play the Ishin
members.

So far taken characters are: Hijikata, Ito, Miki(Suzuki), Saito, and
Kondo. Right now the rest of the Shinsengumi are fair game, so chose
quickly!

Make sure you confirm the character is still open before you register
with LiveJournal, since you'll want to do so under the character's
name. Once you're registered with the community, please refrain from
making posts until the game is officially underway. We will also have
a seperate mailing list for OOC discussions and questions on the RPG.

Thanks,

-MissBehavin/Hijikata
