Indeed I am an historian. I'm an East Asian Studies major, focusing on
Japanese history, although I have an active interest in world history, as
well. My recommendation to aspiring historians is to study historiography
first, and if they are doing an area study like I am, learn to read, write,
and speak the language of the area. I can NOT stress enough how helpful that
it's been to know Japanese...

If you have any more questions, just feel free to contact me privately. It's
my mission to spread history, especially Japanese history, and make it
accessible to all.

Oh...and if I could sum all this up in one sentence, it would be "Keep the
bigger picture in mind."
