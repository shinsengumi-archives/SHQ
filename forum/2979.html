Fujita Goro Memorial

by secretarytocapt3

Every year around this time in Aizu Wakamatu City in Fukushima
Prefecture there is a memorial for Fujita Goro who died on September
28, 1915. The family even over time has become more "public".

Here is a new pic
http://www.aizu.com/shinsengumi/archives/2006/10/post_194.html

Photo at the Fujita family grave (where ashes are stored).
Left is the president of the Hino Shinsengumi Club. The far right is
the president of the Aizu Shinsengumi Club. The middle is a
descendent of Fujita Goro through his second son Tsuyoshi. If any one
can help double check the Japanese I'd appreciate it.
