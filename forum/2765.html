Fans in Japan Updates

by secretarytocapt3

There are always fun activities going on in Japan in relation to the
Shinsengumi and it is totally necessary to watch their messageboards
and blogs because frequently they will post things you will not find
even if you buy every single book on the Shinsengumi. Often these
fans will network extensively and speak to people who work at museums
or with local Shinsengumi "officials" (fans who've spent years
specializing in certain fields).

A list is here
http://www.shinsengumihq.com/links.htm

If you nifty their boards/blogs you'll get alot of neat info...however
not all info can be verified...you have to ask yourself...so where did
they hear this or what book is this from, who did they speak to and so
forth. If they don't mention a book or page number, there information
is not repeated on http://www.shinsengumihq.com ... only a few of us
knows Japanese or have access to reliable human translators, if we
post everything we find without double checking chances are we are
hurting Shinsengumi research in the long run.
