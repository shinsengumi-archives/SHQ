Description

A companion list to the Miburo group founded mainly because our original files section is overflowing with great information on the Shinsengumi in general and Saitou Hajime in particular.

We have many knowledgeable and talented members who entertain and educate us with the information they've culled from their own research.

Although we believe our interest in Saitou and the Shinsengumi is an honorable, living tribute to these brave men, we aren't above slipping into Otaku mode now and then and having fundoshi fantasies and plotting ways to clone and ravish them. *leering hentai grin*
