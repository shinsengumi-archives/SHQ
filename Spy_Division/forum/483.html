Sakura Wars/Shinsengumi Crossover

by shimazuryu

Since the anime "Sakura Wars" takes place in the Taisho era, and since
Nagakura and Saitou (among others) lived until then, I just wanted to
announce my intention to write a crossover story-- probably with
Shinguuji Sakura talking to an elderly Nagakura in one of the parks in
Tokyo that they both seem to frequent.

And maybe a kendo lesson or two ^_^ Since I like Sakura AND Nagakura
so very very much.
