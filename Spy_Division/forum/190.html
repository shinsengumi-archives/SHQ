by spiritus_saitou

A Mary Sue is a story in which the author places herself as the love interest of the main character. She may or may not use her real name, but it's a way of living a fantasy. Often, they're perfect in every way, can solve anything, and the lead guy just can't live without her (among other traits). Someone else may have a better definition, but that'll give you some idea.

Don't ever hesitate to ask anything... some of us have been around for 30 years and still have to ask, "what the heck is that?" :-D

phil (who may be writing Mary Sue in disguise... as Sano?)

by crouching_tiger_realm

Basically, it's an original character that is better than life itself, she/he has all the males pinning for her, is the most selfless creature around the universe, and despite an anguished past, has no (or so light) flaws. He/she is loved by everybody, and at the end of the fic, he/she saves the day sacrificing him/herself (technically May Sue is the female, John Stu the male version). However, what I think they mean in Anime-fiction. net is a fic containing ANY kind of OC. I refuse to accept such a concept since not very OC is a Mary Sue.
Read this to have an idea of what a Mary Sue is: http://www.geocities.com/sheshat/fanfic1/marysue.htm
http://enterprise.mathematik.uni-essen.de/~bastian/Ranma/MarySue.html
Hope this helps
Firuze
