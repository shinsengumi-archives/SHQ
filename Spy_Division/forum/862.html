I wanted a really awesome gallery (but likely our server won't be
happy with another format for just images) but this was the fastest
solution...simply create thumbnails and resize images.

Please feel free to send us anything *original* or fanart related
(manga, comic strip, parodies, sculptures, digital art)...anything as
long as it's not "adult" related (e.g. art which shows blatant
sexuality)...(we are not averse to yaoi/yuri however this gallery is
for public viewing and we have to be sure we aren't exposing kids to
mature material).

and we'll link to your image on dev-art so people can leave you
messages and you get more traffic. I did this for S-girl's
Shinsengumi art (if you visit her gallery you will find more Rurouni
Kenshin fanart too http://s-girl.deviantart.com/)
