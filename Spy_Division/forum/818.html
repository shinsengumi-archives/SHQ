Shinoda-san, omatase itashimashita

The "Kyokuchu Gohatto" (roughly meaning "Unit Prohibitions")-- the 5 rules--
are quoted by many people. Kikuchi Akira-sensei, in his groundbreaking
analytical work "Shinsengumi Hyakuichi no Nazo," states, based on evidence
he has gathered, that the name "Kyokuchu Gohatto" and the articles
themselves, were a creation of the novelist Shimozawa Kan. Shimozawa Kan did
speak to some former Shinsengumi, as well as to Yamakawa Kenjiro, the Aizu
retainer devoted to historical preservation, who was a friend of Saitou
Hajime (Fujita Goro)....but the rules themselves appear to have been
Shimozawa's exercise of creative license.

You said your teacher was a professor at a popular university in
Japan....could he by any chance be the same Kikuchi Akira?

-M.
