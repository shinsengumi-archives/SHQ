by sherlinelee

I'll just say I love Harada the most as he seems , in a good number of incarnations, a pretty benevolent guy who was entirely himself the whole time.

But as the NHK drama is most fresh in my mind -- I rather like Kondou and Yamanami. I was particularly floored by Yamanami's slyness at a critical juncture for the Roshigumi (*cough* Serizawa), but also the circumstances and decisions that led up to his willing seppuku in the NHK version.

As for Kondou, he changed substantially over the course of the 49 episodes and it was very clear he was the reason for so many people of various backgrounds and subtly different feelings and even subtly different ideologies to come together; and it's something I appreciate.
