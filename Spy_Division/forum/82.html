Shinsengumi Fiction Database

by secretarytocapt3

I started a Shinsengumi Fiction Database for stories stored in
fanfiction.net or elsewhere on the net. Everyone can add records so
pitch in and gather the best fiction in one place:)

If you are a writer on ff.net please list your Shinsengumi/Saitou
stories:)

Due to the overwhelming number of RK stories on ff.net the Shinsengumi
kind of get "lost" and so it is absolutely necessary to bring these
stories to the forefront.

In the future, when time permits we can probably convert the database
to html/links for the SHQ page.
