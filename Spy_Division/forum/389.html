Best Versions of The Shinsengumi Characters

by darkmalik

Here's a question which version (anime, manga, live action) of each
character is best. Here's my vote:
Hajime Saitou: PMK version, RK one is nice too but the PMK version is
more softer and I like that.
Souji Okita: PMK version hands down, I don't liket he way they
portrayed him in PK as a stupid looking little kid.
Susumu Yamazaki: PMK I've only seen him in this particular series so
I vote this version best.
Keisuke Yamanami: PMK again as I've only seen him in this series I
can only vote for this version of him.
Isami Kondou: PMK I don't liek the way he's sometimes portrayed as
old and fat in live action so I choose PMK for the liveliness they
gave him in it.
Sanosuke Harada: PMK because of the comedy factor totally.
Toshizo Hijikata: PMK I don't like how they prtray him as an
overpowering jerk with no sense of humor in the live actions I've
seen so I have to vote for the cool one in the anime PMK.
Shinpachi Nagakura: PMK for the comedy routine he, Sanosuke and
Heisuke get into.
Heisuke Toodou: Is he even real? Oh well since I've only seen him in
PMK I vote for the PMK version.
Well here's my votes, what're yours?
