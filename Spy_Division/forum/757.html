Takasugi Shinsaku's death poem:

(in Japanese) "Omnoshiroki/ koto mo naki /yo wo omoshiroku"

(my rendition of it in English) "To make interesting an otherwise
uninteresting world..."

Death poems are supposed to be waka/tanka length, but this one is only haiku
length, signifying, as Watsuki Nobuhiro said in his description of Takasugi,
"Like his life, the poem has a beginning but no real end."

-M.
