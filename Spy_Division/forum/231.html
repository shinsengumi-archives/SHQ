Calling All Poets! "Rurouni Rhythm" Needs YOU!

by unsafesects

Hello Everyone!

I've started a new collaborative project called "Rurouni Rhythm".

This "story" will be a re-telling (summarization) of the Rurouni
Kenshin tale, episode by episode, in verse!

I'm looking for guest authors (I actually hope to have about 50% of it
written by others), and I was certainly hoping that the SHQ Spy
Division would have some interested parties, at the very least, for
Episodes 29-32, or other episodes where Saitou is a prominent figure.

If your entry is selected, it will be posted under my username in the
"Rurouni Rhythm" story. HOWEVER, -full- credit will be given to the
author!

Rules are as follows:

Submissions for episodes can be sent to: mindglue@....

1) The email subject must include the episode number and title. We
will not accept submissions by review.

2) The author's email address, ff.net username, and URL of the
author's profile must be in the email. These will be included in the
chapter if your entry is picked to appear as a guest author in
"Rurouni Rhythm".

3) Poems -must- be sent in .doc or .txt format.

4) There is no guarantee that your poem will be used. Period.

5) Poems may be of any format, from serious to humorous, from haiku to
Dr. Seuss, sonnet to epic Homerian style. However, preference will be
given to humorous poems, as I wish "Rurouni Rhythm" to be, overall,
lighthearted.

6) At this time, we are only looking for poems summarizing episodes
through the end of the Kyoto arc. Feel free to send any episode number
up to episode 65 at any time.

---------------
The first chapter of "Rurouni Rhythm" can be found here:
http://www.fanfiction.net/read.php?storyid=1848493

I hope some of you will consider submitting poems to become guest
poets!
