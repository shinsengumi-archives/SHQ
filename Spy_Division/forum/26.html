LJ Community and Documents Uploaded

by spiritus_saitou

The LJ community, SHQ Spy Office, is up and running. The address is
http://www.livejournal.com/~shq_spy_office/ . You don't have to be
an LJ user or a member of the community to look at the site in
general... you just can't read any of the posts (except for my LJ-
user guinea pig, Masayoshi, who's already a member). If you can,
please contact me ASAP. And by posts I don't necessarily mean the
main entries. If you see an entry, try getting to the good stuff
when you click on the link to get what hides behind the lj-cut. If
you can read either of the stories that are posted there, let me know
ASAP.

More details about what's going on, including guidelines and help,
are in the document I'll be uploading into List Business on the LJ
Community after I post this. I'm sure a notification will be sent to
the list announcing it's been uploaded. Please be sure to read this
document before you start sending stories up to the LJ. Please??? :-)

And, you'll get notification that the Main guidelines have been re-
uploaded. In working with archiving, it became necessary to add a
line to the header to create key words that will search for stories.
It's EXTREMELY limited, but better than nothing. For now, we're
limited to 5 key words that use only 40 characters and I'm making
certain demands on them. I want a pen-name, the source material, and
the rating. For the other two, I'd like to see either the
Shinsengumi as a whole or a specific member who's most prominent in
the story for one of the key words and lastly, an indication of genre
so people looking for action, romance, angst, yaoi, whatever, can
find things by that.

Thanks to Masayoshi for helping me and for sending me the makoto
flag. I'm going to bed! I hope...
