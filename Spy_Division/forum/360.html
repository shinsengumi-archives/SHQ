I uploaded my latest fanart of Saitou in Photos in the "illustrations"
folder. Its of RK SAitou reclining influenced ofcourse by our
favorite scene of Kenshin and Misao imagining Tokio to be Kannon, the
goddess of mercy. My fanart also has a cat based on Angrybee's ff.net
story Hajime & Tokio, and the current RPG at
http://www.livejournal.com/community/miburo_lair
We all associate the Shinsengumi with wolves and stuff but I was just
imagining RK Saitou with a cat and I found it to be very sexy.

Before you take a look at the pic imagine yourself to be Tokio...
it was a long day at working teaching young ladies, doing
administrative office stuff. You come home with a list of things to
do, make dinner, bring in the laundry, pick up the kids, take care of
the kids...and feed the cat..."where is the cat?" you wonder. Then
you here the faint sound of a bottle knocked over in the other room...
you open the shoji and....well...so much for the quiet and peaceful
night at home....
