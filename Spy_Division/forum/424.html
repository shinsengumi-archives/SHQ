THIRD ENTRY from our resident artist...
***please post comments at
http://p076.ezboard.com/fshinsengumiheadquartersfrm8.showMessage?

Entry
http://1happyturtle.com/makoto/Saitohworship.jpg

Description from the artist...
On the left is the front view - the collar is raised to protect our
dear wolf's neck and head from the chilly nights, and the bronze
shoulder guards will keep him from being harmed immediately if an
enemy attacks from behind
to chop at his shoulder, there is also a steel-plated armor covering
for the chest. The word for "makoto" is embossed so that the word
pops out (as shown in the side view). He can wear wheatever shirt
and pants over the plate, so
I didn't draw them. The swords are contained in a "dual shield" so
there would be less hassle when trying to tie both swords to the
waist. It is basically a sheath with two openings for two swords.
The back view - the word "makoto" is printed on the back in black.
Side view - here's a clearer
picture of the chest armor. Cloth is attached to the sides so that
it can be tied to his chest around the back. It is also lined with
soft cloth on the inside so that, at night, the cold steel would not
touch our hero's chest (and abs) and make him shiver. The shape is
curved to fit over a chest, so
that it can be worn comfortably. There is also a difference in the
peak design - here, they are more in the form of ascending mountain
peaks, instead of a flat mountain range design.

And that's it! ^^ Thanks so much~!!!
