There's always one more thing...

by spiritus_saitou

Thanks to Barb mentioning something about keeping text and image
files separate on the SHQ site, I realized I'd made a fatal error...
nothing new, I do it all the time. I had image folders in the files
section and she's absolutely right about wanting to keep them
independent. SOOOO... In the photos section you will find various
albums all sitting clean and new and ready to take images. Just so
we can find things, the albums are:

Doujinshi scans --- images from doujinshi published by non-members
you find delightful or inspiring.

Doujinshi Originals --- doujinshi created by members of the list.

Manga Scans --- same as for doujinshi, but for manga.

Manga Originals --- manga created by members.

Illustrations --- scans of illustrations or calligraphy from books

Screencaps --- captures from the various anime, films, and other
moving picture formats.

Single Originals --- the one-shots of the art world, pictures created
by members that are meant to stand alone.

Am I leaving out any other formats?

If you have a group of images (posting up an original manga or
doujinshi, for instance, would be in several separate uploads),
create an album within the album it belongs in, giving your album the
title of the piece.

In the next few days, the guidelines will change again... this time
oriented towards art. For now, I'm going to try to leave you guys
alone, stop bugging you. :-)
