Upload of SaiSa stories to Fiction Archive under Files

by spiritus_saitou

I've uploaded all of my completed stories to the yahoo site (and Barb
has already started transfering story files to the SHQ website). If
you're interested, I have three stories in progress available at
http://www.fanfiction.net/~philalexandros .

"Kitaku" (rated R) follows two timelines: 15 years after Sano has to
leave Japan (post OAV "Reflections") and flashbacks to 1878/79.
Saitou finds the mature Sano on the mainland and they begin a new
phase in their association.

"Oujougiwa no Seizonsha" (rated R) should probably be "Seizonsha no
Oujougiwa" (only our resident Japanese translator knows for
sure... ). Anyway, it's a rough one with Saitou having to bring Sano
through opium withdrawal when the ahou decides he's had enough of
living in the Meiji Era.

And "Yasou" (rated R), which I posted the intro to here on the list.
I got part of Chapter One written before we began this expansion
project for miburo and I haven't been able to touch it since. I'll
continue to post installments of this story on the list.

I'm very much RK and SaiSa obsessed, so that's likely the most you'll
see from me... though Barb's still trying her best to talk me into a
SaitouxTokio story. I'm about to be introduced to PMK... who knows
where my yaoi tendencies will lead me there. And most have you have
probably already been introduced to these stories. I'm just making
them available here (and the website) for the future. This is
Shinsengumi Headquarters, after all.

Masayoshi's already regaling us with art in the Photos section.
Yeah! The rest of you --- get those fingers saving files to htm/html
(just *what* is the difference between htm and html... one text, one
images??) and upload those babies!
