Dear Chuck
YES! The Rokumeikan was in the last scenes of Lady Snowblood! (^_^)
It was scandalous on many levels to the people of the time...women
dancing with foreigners and so forth. The newspapers had a field day
after each event. The Rokumeikan was also a feature film directed by
KON ICHIKAWA and a play written by YUKIO MISHIMA.

I highly recommend fanfic writers to look into the
Rokumeikan---history provides some pretty interesting backdrops to
stories.
