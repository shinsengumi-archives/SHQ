FIC PREVIEW: Rokugatsu no Yume

A preview scene from "Rokugatsu no Yume" (A Dream of the Sixth Moon),
the fic I'm going to write and release on the anniversary of Ikedaya--
July 6th (by our calendar, not the old Imperial calendar):

---

Harada leaned on his spear, exasperated by the wait. Turning to Okita,
he grinned nervously.

"Neh, Okita-kun, did you remember to wash your fundoshi?"

"H-Harada-san?!"

Harada suddenly felt a stealthy tapping on his shoulder. Turning
around he saw Saitou, making final adjustments to his armor
chestplate. "I've made sure to wash mine, Harada-kun. Very well, I
might add."

---

Just a little something to look forward to ^_^

--M.
