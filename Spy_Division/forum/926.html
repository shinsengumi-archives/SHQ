Okita Art

http://1happyturtle.com/makoto/Okitaabstract.jpg (no scanner -_-)
the drawing is inspired by a Shinsengumi doll I received for Christmas
and while the face is so sweet like how Okita is often portrayed in
modern fiction I looked closely and saw that the figure has Kondo's crest!
I was looking down on the doll as I was drawing and so there is an
abstract feel to the work and I liked the details of the hair, which really
was that long on the doll (^_^).
