Courtesy of Safety:

After some hard work we're pleased to announce that Shinsengumifics
is now active and ready to go!

Shinsengumifics.com is an original and fan fiction achive in the
model of other archive sites such as fanfiction.net where authors
can upload and edit stories but unlike ff.net it allows for more
than just Rurouni Kenshin and Peacemaker Kurogane-based stories.
Any Shensengumi universe - RK to the NHK drama, Kaze Hikaru to pure
historical - is open for all authors to unleash their creativity and
tell some great stories about the Shinsengumi.

So come by, register and add your stories! Or come by and read and
offer comments/encouragement to other fic writers - like ff.net
there is a capacity to submit and recieve reviews but unlike ff.net
you may add images (limited). We look forward to seeing you there.
