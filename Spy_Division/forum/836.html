Hello,

If you would like a detailed plot bunny for a story just download
this word document
http://1happyturtle.com/Hosting/FIREINKYOTO.doc

Keep in mind that Saitou in this story is original and not based on
RK, PMK, NHK or Mibugishiden.

In fact I made the story "inspired by" the Shinsengumi so the name
Shinsengumi was changed to Ookami Gumi and Aizu-han is Ise-han
because ofcourse this is a work of fiction and many situations were
completely created.

If you really this plot bunny and will write it as original fic post
to this mailing list so people know that you will officially adopt
the plot bunny--and *ofcourse* just adapt, delete it anyway your
heart desires. I marked certain parts as {climactic scene #1} or
{sensual} etc because in doing so I am able to see the flow of the
story and see if it has something going on for the reader...that's
how I plan things. Many script writers also do this as a way of
insuring that their work has something movie makers can work with.

However people who are just curious are more than welcome to skim the
plot. I have a new story for every moment of the day involving
Saitou---however most are comedies set when he is older (^_^)

I have alot of ideas, but no time, or I don't want to waste time
writing like...a really really long story no one will read. My prose
is really for non-fiction. To have someone write non-fiction write
fiction is like having a swimmer who swims laps in a pool do
underwater ballet. It's similar but not the same (^_^).

I know there are alot of talented authors and artists here who can
write great stories for fanfiction.net and get alot of reviews.

So if you have nothing better to do over the summer give it a shot!
