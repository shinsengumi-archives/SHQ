Re: [SHQ_Spy_Division] Give me Music Recommendations...

by chickhasstick

Well, a few things come to mind, but they are a bit off the beaten track.

1) The Oaf-Big Wreck from In Loving Memory-No real person or theme here, it would just be a great tune to choreograph a fight to.

2) Two tunes performed by Carlos Nunez. The versions I have are from the album Almas de Fisterra. First is 'Un Galicien Libre a Paris'; it is a very positive sounding tune that would be good for any Shinsengumi member who is always very upbeat, i.e. any version of Soji or the NHK and PMK versions of Harada. The second tune is called 'A Noite Pecha', it is a little darker and would do for any member who is more mysterious or introspective. Any Saito in any universe.

3) Sky becomes Blue by Jin Hashimoto is another positive tune, suitable for the sunnier members. It has already been used as a theme for one of the Kamen Rider series....actually I think it was the one with Jo Odagiri in it.

4) Sake in the Jar-This is a interesting spin on the Irish/Scottish folk song 'Whiskey in the Jar' by Akiko Yano and the Chieftains from the album Tears of Stone. Might be good for those members who get themselves into trouble over women (or anything else) on a regular basis. Might be good for the comedian trio of PMK and the NHK Hijikata in the early episodes.

5) The Blood is Strong by Gordie Sampson. Not sure what album this is from, my recording is from a music festival purchase. It is a declaration of a will to survive that might be suitable for the Ichimura brothers of PMK.

6) Marco Polo by Loreena McKennitt from the album Book of Secrets. Not sure why but this makes me think of the scene in Shinsengumi! (NHK) when they ambush the corrupt Osaka official (cannot think of his name at the moment). The action is interspersed with scenes of Oyuki dancing for Kondo.

7) Boy Inside the Man by Tom Cochrane from Victory Day. I have almost finished watching the NHK series and in the last few episodes there seems to be a lot of reminiscing and navel gazing and that is what this song is sort of about. Might also be used for some of the introspection at the end of the Kepporoku series too. Would love to see a video from that series!

8) Any of the movements for the cello by JS Bach as played by Yo Yo Ma.

Not sure how legal it is, but if any of these peak your curiosity I may be able to send a copy to you.

WS
