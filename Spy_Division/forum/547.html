Here are the guidelines as they appear in the group files section



*Submitting Fiction*



Stories and poetry can be posted to the list or uploaded into the
Fiction folder under Files, or both. When you post to the list,
formatting will usually be lost. Saving your story or poem as an html
document enables you to format a story the way you want and have it be
seen that way. Just keep readability in mind when you get enthusiastic
with color and fonts. J (In MSWord, to save a document in html format,
you can either choose the "Save as" feature under File and choose "web
page" in the drop down menu OR again under File, click on "Save as web
document." Those of us accustomed to uploading stories on ff.net should
be aware that uploading to the yahoo list site keeps formatting exactly
as you save it, unlike ff.net which puts lines in after returns. It's
been ages since I've worked with WordPerfect, so I don't know how it
works, but I imagine it's similar. Send a query to the list for help if
you need to.)
