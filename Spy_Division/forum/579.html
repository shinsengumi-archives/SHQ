Kondo's first adopted son, Shuuhei, died in the war, I think. His 2nd
adopted son-- his nephew by blood, Yuugoro-- married Kondou's "real"
daughter Tama. The current descendant of the Miyagawa/Kondo line is Miyagawa
Toyoji, descended from Kondo's brother Miyagawa Otogoro. Hijikata had no
children (they say, but it's not known for sure), but descendants of his
siblings survive. I believe the same goes for Okita.
