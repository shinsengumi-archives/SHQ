by secretarytocapt3

(This message is just an announcement and therefore worksafe)

"Illusion of Love" is a short story which is a reincarnation of a
Peacemaker Kurogane fanfic I did years and years ago. Now, the story
features an original version of the Shinsengumi in a yaoi work and
also some genre-bending elements. I believe yaoi, like any genre can
be a vehicle for other themes which can add diversity to the field.
Illusion of Love does have very minimal man x man and also a thread of
spirituality which will hopefully make the work very unique to readers.

The story is hosted at http://www.samurai-on-samurai.com
Please complete the (free) registration.
You must be 18 years old to join.

Illusion of Love is under the category "Samurai Shorts" and you can't
miss this section of the website because it has male lower torsos
covered in just fundoshi (^_^)

Flames, comments for the story can be sent to secretarytocapt3(at)
hotmail.com

[Yaoi] Sacred Blood

by secretarytocapt3

(This message does not contain yaoi writing or images. This just an
announcement and therefore worksafe.)

I recently completed a short story for Samurai-on-Samurai.com
<http://www.samurai-on-samurai.com/> called "Sacred Blood" which takes
place during feudal times.

A young handsome daimyo, Lord Akodo, is shot with an arrow during an
excursion in the forest. Desperate to save him, the retainers take him
to a mysterious shrine attended by a priestess named Airi. Before Airi
can do anything she requests that the archer be found. "Sacred Blood"
is about sibling rivalry between brothers Hayato, the warrior, and Haru
the healer and advisor and their loyalty to the injured daimyo.

The tale contains blood, very little mxm sexuality and touches of the
paranormal.

Aside from "Sacred Blood", Samurai-on-samurai.com is also hosting
"Illusion of Love" which is an interesting Saitou x Yamazaki story with
a twist.

Just a footnote, Sacred Blood is a short story told by some characters
from, KATANA DUET,
<http://www.bookstrand.com/authors/silapajarun/kd.asp> a novella of
mine which was recently published by Bookstrand.com (the title is
currently on sale at 15% off so check it out) (^_^).

I'm already planning another Shinsengumi story for Samurai-on-Samurai.
