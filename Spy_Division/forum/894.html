[Parody] Real Samurai of Genius

by secretarytocapt3

It's one of these commercials:
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZHNt9rXSsTM
which has a narrator and singer alternating--the singer's words are in
(parenthesis).


Sake light presents: Real samurai of genius

(real samurai of genius)

Today we salute you Serizawa Kamo

(Mr. I solve all my problems with violence)

You invent new ways to use an iron fans and won't take any sh*t from
sumo wrestlers

(He has anger management issues)

Only a man named 'Wild Duck' like Serizawa would dare to go fishing in
a temple pond

(those stupid koi deserve to die)

Some may ask, is Serizawa-sensei a suitable leader for a group of
warriors?

(everyone listens to a man who uses a cannon in the city)

It takes episodes of insanity to do what you do Serizawa Kamo, but it
takes more guts to do it and think you'll get away with it. So down a
bottle of sake, mighty chief of heavy artillery---this one's for you.
