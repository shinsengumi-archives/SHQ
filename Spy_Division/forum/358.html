hellooooo Saitohworship
I would like to comment on each pic.
Saitoh Hajime B&W...believe me I know how hard it is to draw Saitoh's
eyes and jawline...its easy to make him to fat to skinny or too "edgy"
but you got him just right.....also even without the advantage of
using thick lines around his eyes, you still managed to capture those
sexy eyes we all love. I think you did a great job on his hair too!

Nagakura's Portrait...as good as Nanae Kurono! This may sound odd but
I appreciated the detail of the bandage it actually looks like the
bandage is "on" his nose...you added some dimension there. I like
your way of highlighting his eyes too. Simple yet effective in
capturing the mischievous nature of the PMK's capt. of the 2nd squad.

Harada's Portrait is interesting because to me it looks like Harada
-before- the Shinsengumi....teenage Harada! and no doubt even MORE
wild than the Harada we know 0_0

Thank you for sharing your art. I think other members may have
emailed you privately or something but I wanted to post my comments here.
