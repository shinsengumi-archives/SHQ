New Saitou-Sano musicvid

by spiritus_saitou

I kept expecting secretarytocapt3 to say something about this since she was the one who brought it to my attention. (This is what I get for ignoring LJ lately).

There's a new RK musicvid out by a very talented person. It's called "I Hate Everything About You" and it's by schwarefee (better known as kuroiyousei, aka Black Faery). Extremely well done... I really don't expect anything less than excellent from her in anything she tries her hand at. It's her first musicvid and she's really looking for some feedback and any encouragement to keep putting time into making musicvids. Yeah, it's yaoi-inspired, but doesn't have to be taken in that perspective. It's as much about the tentative friendship between two opposites who really aren't opposites at all.

http://www.animemusicvideos.org/members/members_videoinfo.php?v=39760

Please take a look and give kuroiyousei an honest opinion of the piece. She's a wonderful person who puts a lot of time and love into making us RK Saitou fans happy.
