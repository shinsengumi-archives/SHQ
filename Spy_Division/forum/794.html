Saitou Hajime Fanart

by genkigeeks

My latest drawing, a tribute to Saito Hajime. This digital painting
was based on a black-and-white manga-style drawing of Saito at the
back of a doujinshi called Spark! (sadly, I don't know who the artist
was). Even though it was simply a stark monochrome image, I was
captivated. I just had to paint my own version of it.

http://www.deviantart.com/deviation/37693516/

The next drawing I'm working on is of Saitou in his Shinsengumi
uniform (as he's seen in Rurouni Kenshin). I'm also thinking of making
Souji Okita (Peacemaker Kurogane) art. ^_^

Thank you for your time! I hope you enjoy my work!
