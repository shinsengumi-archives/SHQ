THE ITOU AFFAIR hits the Shinsengumi Radio Airwaves

by secretarytocapt3

"...Shinsengumi radio, all makoto, all the time..."

the following audio drama was performed by Hirotada Tokugawa (M) the
MAN OF MANY VOICES (^_^)

the sound engineer was Harada Sanosuke

the host is Nagakura Shinpachi

there will be a very very mysterious caller...hmmm who can it be?

we know that he accompanied Itou-sensei and Toudou Heisuke

he likes soba and even had a meal at the Akabeko soba restaurant
(*akabeko are those toy buffalo with moving heads, also a folkcraft of
the Aizu region)

and he goes by "#3" as he desperately attempts to relate Itou-sensei's
plot to the the utterly clueless Nagakura

there will be a mention of the Satsuma Public Pay Phone Service which
attempts to push its phone cards of Great Saigo (Saigo Kichinosuke or
TAKAMORI, Sakamoto Ryoma and Katsura-sensei)

we hope you enjoy this fanwork and ****please**** consider giving us
comments and suggestions and YES! Shinsengumi radio does invite flame
mail!!! In fact, if you are lucky, Hijikata and Kondou may even answer
your email "on the air" in a future broadcast (I cannot guarantee that
these two DJs will give a straight answer). There is nothing more
fulfilling for a fan to get feedback on his work. And just tell us if
you want to go by a certain pen name in your correspondence to the
radio station. I believe our resident voice actor may consider
scripts as well (please include notes on dramatic direction as a guide).

Please right-click and save the file to your computer
https://mywebspace.wisc.edu/tnamwong/web/NagakuraonKH.mp3
[8.43 MB]

This outstanding piece of voice acting has not yet been post to
http://www.shinsengumihq.com/MFD.htm because I'm trying to finish
another broadcast---a very rated PG-13 edition when Madame Matsu from
Shimabara's HOUSE OF PLEASURE AND PAIN goes after a customer who is
late paying his bills........
