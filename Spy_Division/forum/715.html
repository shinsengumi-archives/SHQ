I think the most fascinating pair in NHK's SHINSENGUMI!
was by far Serizawa and Oume.

Wasn't it romantic when she asked where she would be buried?
The Wild Duck said "join me in my grave".

That was sweet!

Ok, maybe not.

But still I think they were very dynamic (sort of like Shishio and Yume).

by hajimenokizu

I definitely agree. Theirs was a very unique relationship, not the
fluffy kind or the shy type. It was a nice breath of fresh air in my
opinion. It was funny how they understood each other and showed it
in not so -normal- ways. I mean who says she doesn't know what will
happen to her after she dies? And her knight in shining armor comes
along and says join me. LOL! While I re-watched that I got
goosebumps.

Oh Sec... Saitou was there overhearing the conversation. I'm not
sure if it's just me but... I have this nagging feeling that SK was
saying that not only for Oume but for Saitou to hear it (hopefully
to fulfil that wish)? Wasn't it at that point that SK already knew
he would be dying soon, which is why he was so open to Oume about
it? LOL!
