Doujinshi Cover as Fanart

by secretarytocapt3

I was over at http://www.hajimenokizu.com
.wait. you've never BEEN TO HNK? well go and check it out
The charm of doujinshi is the many renditions of your favorite
characters from all different "universes"

so I was thinking...
what if fans made up their own doujinshi?---maybe not an ENTIRE
doujinshi but just the cover...

THE FOURTH NAME
covers the bio of Saitou between the time he dropped the name
Yamaguchi Jiro to when he starts using Fujita Goro
the fourth name is ***Ichinohe Denpachi so the story is post Aizu
defeat to when he gets married and uses the name Fujita

just to make this a doujinshi for women ofcourse there will be YASO
and Tokio....you know to make things "interesting" (look if Japanese
artists can pair up Saitou with Hiko then I can do anything)

please use your imagination pretend it is all colorful and glossy and
real professional
I dislike facial hair on guys---but since this is Saitou as a prisoner
of war in perpetual kinshin---then he's got to have a beard
warning: Saitou is a "pretty boy" (^_^)
http://1happyturtle.com/temporary/4thname.jpg

note: I was reading some posts in Japanese Aizu fandom and finally
someone came up with a wacky idea "Takagi Goro/Morinusuke as Byakkotai
Ninja"

*Takagi MOrinusuke was Tokio's younger brother (age 14/15 in 1868 so
technically he was a bit too young to be in the Byakkotai)
he was just a messenger and scout during the siege...but he -can- be a
ninja in camouflage---I drew him in the top box

The men (young and old) were all sent to Inawashiro temporarily.
Teens were then sent to Edo (Tokyo)

places including Tokyo but in the case of SAitou, Takada and then
finally Aomori (in the north).
Teens like the other characters I drew -stayed- in Edo/Tokyo and
attended school while living in prison camps there.

Takagi Goro/ MOrinusuke (Tokio's brother)....so I see him having an
