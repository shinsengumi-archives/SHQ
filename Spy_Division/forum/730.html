Fujita Goro walks home with a big box. He has a scowl on his face
because ~someone~ insists that he needs to work overtime to bring in
more money. Unfortunately the only open security position aside from
his undercover work is a post at the Rokumeikan dance hall which will
have special Christmas dance for the barbarians...errr...foreigners
working in Tokyo.

As he entered his home's gate his niece Yamaguchi Yukiko staggers out
the front door gasping...

Goro lifts his eyebrow, "Yukiko! Are you ill what's wrong?"

Yukiko, turning blue, "C-c-can't breathe---corset suffocating me..."
his niece takes shallow breaths, pulls at the lace collar from her
throat and walks out of the Fujita residence in a fancy western dress.

Goro steps inside the living room, careful to avoid stepping on the
measuring tape, scissors and pins on the wooden floor. Goro "Tokio
why have you applied this corset torture to my niece?"

Tokio "Everyone student at the Normal School needs to dress in western
clothing for special events---and you HAVE to wear a corset to enhance
one's figure---what is in the box?"

Goro "clothing I must wear to work at the Rokumeikan--I don't
understand why barbarians where layers upon layers of clothing!"

Tokio rummages through the box and helps him dress...

Tokio "oh a perfect fit! Wait right here, I made myself a dress at
the school's workshop from some odd and end scraps."

Tokio comes out dressed nicely...
