Aoi

by shimazuryu

Under Photos>Single Originals is my entry into the Moeyoken Fanart
contest-- Tokugawa Yoshinobu's daughter, Aoi. Note-- she's wearing a
velvet choker around her neck, with a silver pendant in the center
bearing the Tokugawa crest.

Revised information:

Name: Tokugawa Aoi (also Aoi-hime)
Age: 22
Primary martial schooling: Edo-ha Yagyu Shinkage Ryu
Weapons trained in: Katana/wakizashi, tanto, naginata, Smith & Wesson
revolver, and kunai.
Weapons carried: tanto in obi knot, (sometimes) revolver in large
sleeves, and kunai...in certain places ;)
Character: Aristocratic-- deliciously, seductively arrogant. Enjoys
the attention she gets from wearing her kimono top loose. Often has
Matsudaira and Kondou's daughters over for visits, tea ceremony, etc.
When she trains in martial arts without a teacher (except with the
revolver), she trains in the dark.
