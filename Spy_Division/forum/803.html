Dear Sec,

I've loved this site for so long - I was convinced
that you guys already knew about it! So I'd be happy
to answer some of the questions.

Saitou is not necessarily PMK influenced - although
his hairstyle seems like it is. However, Saitou in
this fan-universe is portrayed as a retainer of Aizu
(Yamaguchi Hajime) who is sent to work for the
Shinsengumi as an insider.

The site is very much a Toshihime-Katamori 'ship, as
well as Saitou-Tokio and Yamanami-Akesato. So, the
pics of Saitou with a woman are Saitou-Tokio. There
are a few where he is messing with Toshihime, and only
one where it's implied that the woman is Yaso:
http://gasi2dou.moo.jp/35555.jpg
The theme is "Tonami-han" and the caption for Saitou
reads "the mysterious local wife", which might not
make any sense but "genchi-zuma (local wife)" is not
exactly a word with positive connotations.
There are a few others with the Joushitai - but my
personal favourites are the ones of Teru Hime and
Tokio.
Also, Yamakawa is depicted here as Ookura. And there
are a few with his younger brother too.

I seriously thought that everyone else knew about this
site already - I'd be happy to try and translate some
of the comic strips and the character descriptions. It
really, really cracks me up.

But I must get back to work now - hope I helped answer
some of the questions.

Best wishes,

Mio.
